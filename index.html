DAWheadz is a free, open, place, to share info, ideas, and knowledge about digital music production.

Enter Forum

A DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) is a piece of software which allows you to edit audio files and music. Examples of popular DAWs are: Ableton Live, Logic Pro, Reaper, Pro Tools, FL Studio, Garage Band. The forum is dedicated to all discussion around all topics related to music creation and production.
